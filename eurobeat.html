Eurobeat - Initial D's Soundtrack

Eurobeat is a high-energy electronic music genre that plays a pivotal role in Initial D, amplifying the intensity of its iconic racing scenes and defining the anime's distinct atmosphere. Originating in Europe and gaining immense popularity in Japan, eurobeat is characterized by fast-paced beats, catchy melodies, and vibrant vocals. Each important scene in Initial D is paired with a memorable eurobeat track, making it easy to associate key moments with their signature songs.
